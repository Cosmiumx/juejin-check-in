# Privacy Policy for Juejin Daily Companion

**Last Updated**: January 16, 2026

## Overview

Juejin Daily Companion ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our Chrome extension.

## Information Collection

**We do NOT collect any personal information.**

The Extension operates entirely on your local device and does not transmit any data to external servers.

## Local Data Storage

The Extension uses Chrome's local storage API to store the following information **on your device only**:

### What We Store Locally

1. **Check-in Status**: Whether you have completed your daily check-in
2. **Last Check-in Time**: Timestamp of your most recent check-in
3. **User Preferences**: Your choice of manual or automated reminder mode

### What We Do NOT Store

- Personal identification information
- Browsing history
- Cookies from other websites
- Login credentials
- Any data from websites other than juejin.cn

## How the Extension Works

### Permissions and Their Usage

#### tabs
- **Purpose**: To detect when you are on juejin.cn website
- **Data Access**: Only checks the URL of active tabs
- **No Data Collection**: Does not read or store browsing history

#### storage
- **Purpose**: To save your preferences and check-in status locally
- **Data Location**: Stored only on your device
- **No Data Transmission**: Never sent to any external server

#### activeTab
- **Purpose**: To interact with juejin.cn when you click the extension icon
- **User Control**: Only activates when you explicitly click the extension
- **Limited Scope**: Only accesses the active Juejin tab

#### alarms
- **Purpose**: To provide daily reminder notifications
- **Functionality**: Schedules local notifications only
- **No Tracking**: Does not collect or transmit any data

#### host_permissions (https://juejin.cn/user/*)
- **Purpose**: To function on the Juejin platform
- **Scope**: Limited to juejin.cn user center pages only (/user/*)
- **No Cross-Site Access**: Cannot access other websites or other sections of juejin.cn (homepage, articles, etc.)

## Data Transmission

**The Extension does not transmit any data.**

All operations are performed locally in your browser. There are no:
- Analytics or tracking scripts
- External API calls for data collection
- Third-party service integrations
- Data uploads to any server

## Third-Party Services

This Extension does not use any third-party services, including:
- Analytics platforms (e.g., Google Analytics)
- Advertising networks
- Data collection services
- External APIs (except Juejin.cn itself for functionality)

## User Authentication

The Extension does not handle user authentication. It relies on your existing Juejin.cn login session. We do not:
- Store your Juejin username or password
- Access your Juejin account credentials
- Transmit authentication tokens

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted:
- Your data is protected by your browser's security mechanisms
- No risk of data breaches from our servers (we don't have servers)
- Data is automatically cleared if you uninstall the extension

## User Rights

You have complete control over your data:

### Access Your Data
All data is stored in Chrome's local storage, which you can view through Chrome DevTools.

### Delete Your Data
You can delete all extension data by:
1. Uninstalling the extension
2. Clearing browser data for the extension
3. Resetting the extension in Chrome settings

### Control Permissions
You can:
- Disable the extension at any time
- Revoke permissions through Chrome settings
- Switch between manual and automated reminder modes

## Children's Privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13 years of age. The Extension is designed for general users of the Juejin platform.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

## Transparency

https://github.com/Cosmiumx/juejin-check-in

This Extension complies with:
